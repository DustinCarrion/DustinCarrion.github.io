Dustin Carrión-Ojeda is a Ph.D. student in the Image and Video Analysis Group (IVA) at TU Darmstadt, where he focus on low-shot and multimodal learning under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Sc. Simone Schaub-Meyer. He received his M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence from Université Paris-Saclay in 2022, supervised by Prof. Isabelle Guyon, and his B.Eng. in Information Technology from Yachay Tech University in 2020, advised by Prof. Israel Pineda and Prof. Rigoberto Fonseca-Delgado.
